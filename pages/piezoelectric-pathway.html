Green energy harvesting from human footsteps

A sustainable green energy needs is increasing. Meanwhile the energy itself is not always available compare to the needs according to its sources. Meanwhile, the use of renewable energy source still as low as 12,15% compare to fuel and fossil sources. This will increase as many electric appliances usage keep grow exponentially.
